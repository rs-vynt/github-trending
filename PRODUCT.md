# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Developers and technical leaders looking for trending open source projects across Github. They need to quickly parse historical trends, discover new tools, and understand what the projects actually do.

## Product Purpose
A fast, beautiful, and AI-summarized Github Trending Dashboard. It aggregates daily and monthly github trending data and presents it cleanly, so developers can keep their finger on the pulse of the open-source ecosystem without digging through messy markdown or JSON logs.

## Brand Personality
Premium, smart, focused, developer-centric, minimalist but high-tech.

## Anti-references
- Messy, dense JSON logs
- Cluttered data tables with endless scrolling
- Generic "startup" SaaS templates (no loud 3D illustrations or overly bouncy animations)
- Overuse of Gray/Silver cards on white backgrounds that look like standard Bootstrap/Tailwind defaults.

## Design Principles
- **Readability first**: The content is data, so hierarchy and scannability are paramount.
- **Glass and Dark Mode**: Use sophisticated dark mode with subtle glows and glassmorphism to look cutting-edge, rather than plain flat dark gray.
- **Subtle motion**: Interactions should feel snappy but fluid. Motion is used to reveal information, not just for decoration.
- **Focus over noise**: Avoid distracting elements; let the repository cards and summaries take center stage.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability (WCAG AA).
- Reduced motion support for accessibility.
- Clear structural hierarchy.
